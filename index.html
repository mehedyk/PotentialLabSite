About the Lab

A Hub for Innovation and Research Excellence

Artificial Intelligence Lab

The Artificial Intelligence Lab (AI Lab) was established in Spring 2025, initiated by Sudipto Pramanik, Lecturer at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Daffodil International University. What began as an informal collaboration between curious undergraduate students and their mentor gradually developed into a structured research environment dedicated to advancing modern artificial intelligence.

The lab’s mission is to conduct high-impact research in Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing while nurturing the next generation of researchers. We aim to build a vibrant research ecosystem within the EEE Department at DIU where students actively engage in hands-on research, publish their work in reputed venues, and explore real-world AI applications. Our vision is to foster innovation, curiosity, and interdisciplinary collaboration—empowering students and faculty to create cutting-edge AI solutions that contribute to both technological progress and societal benefit.

Research Areas

Cutting-edge research across multiple domains of artificial intelligence and machine learning

Large Language Models

Fine-tuning and deploying LLMs like GPT, LLaMA, and Mistral for domain-specific applications including email generation, text summarization, and Bengali NLP.

Deep Learning

Advanced neural network architectures for computer vision, natural language understanding, and multimodal AI systems with applications in healthcare and automation.

Reinforcement Learning

Developing intelligent agents using RL techniques including Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) and policy gradient methods for sequential decision-making tasks.

Signal Processing

Digital signal processing for biomedical applications, ECG/PPG analysis, arrhythmia detection, and real-time health monitoring systems.

Explainable AI

Building interpretable machine learning models with transparency and trust, focusing on understanding AI decision-making processes and model behavior.

Research Publications

Publications, Projects & Contributions

An Automatic Spam-Aware Email Generation Scheme

Status: Under Review

Journal: IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems (IEEE TCSS)

Fine-tuned GPT-2 and Mistral-7B using LoRA and SFT to generate professional emails that evade spam filters. Achieved superior performance in context, safety, and spam avoidance.

Leveraging Direct Preference Optimization (DPO)

Status: Under Review

Journal: IEEE Access

Applied DPO on LLaMA-3.2-1B using Kaggle Bengali dataset. Generated concise, human-like headlines with strong contextual alignment.

Ventricular Fibrillation Detection in ECG

Status: Published

Journal: Signal, Image and Video Processing (Springer)

TCSC algorithm on MIT-BIH & CU databases. Achieved 80% sensitivity, 83% specificity. Real-time clinical applicability.

PPG Signal Analysis for AF Detection

Status: Undergraduate Thesis

Institution: BUET

ML-based PPG analysis for AF detection. Feature extraction and classification using SVM, RF, and LSTM techniques.
