About Me

Lecturer, Researcher & AI Enthusiast

Sudipto Pramanik

Lecturer & Research Mentor

Department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering
Daffodil International University

Sudipto Pramanik is a dedicated lecturer and researcher with a strong foundation in AI and machine learning. He holds an MSc in EEE (Communication Signal Processing) from BUET and is committed to bridging theory and practice.

His work spans deep learning, NLP (especially Bengali), signal processing, and explainable AI. He mentors students through IEEE and conducts workshops on ML and MATLAB.

With a passion for innovation, Sudipto aims to develop intelligent systems that solve real-world challenges while nurturing the next generation of engineers.

Research Works

Publications, Projects & Contributions

An Automatic Spam-Aware Email Generation Scheme Based on Efficient Fine-Tuning of Large Language Models

Under Review: IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems (IEEE TCSS)

Fine-tuned GPT-2 and Mistral-7B using LoRA and SFT to generate professional emails that evade spam filters. Achieved superior performance in context, safety, and spam avoidance.

Leveraging Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) for Enhanced Abstractive Summarization in Bangla News Headline Generation

Under Review: IEEE Access

Applied DPO on LLaMA-3.2-1B using Kaggle Bengali dataset. Generated concise, human-like headlines with strong contextual alignment.

A Simple Time Domain Algorithm for the Detection of Ventricular Fibrillation in Electrocardiogram

Published: Signal, Image and Video Processing (Springer)

TCSC algorithm on MIT-BIH & CU databases. Achieved 80% sensitivity, 83% specificity. Real-time clinical applicability.

Photoplethysmograph (PPG) Signal Analysis Using Machine Learning for Atrial Fibrillation Detection

Undergraduate Thesis, BUET

ML-based PPG analysis for AF detection. Feature extraction and classification using SVM, RF, and LSTM.
